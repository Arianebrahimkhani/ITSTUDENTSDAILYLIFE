About Us

We are a group of Applied Computer Science students at Thomas More University in Geel, Belgium. Our website, IT Students Daily Life, is designed to give new students insights into what it's like to be an IT student.

Through our videos, we share information about our daily routines and experiences, as well as insights into the courses in our schedule. We hope to help new students get a better understanding of the IT program at Thomas More University.
